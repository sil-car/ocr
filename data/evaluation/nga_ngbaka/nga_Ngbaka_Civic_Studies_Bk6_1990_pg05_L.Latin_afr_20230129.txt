38. Mɛ kɛ cḭtoyen wia we dɛ ma
(Les d̍evoirs du citoyen)

1. Ma wia na be Zaire dɑ᷆e mbula ngɔ nı̀i  ꞌda
a, ꞌnɛ a d̄ɔnggɔ on̄wa ngɔ le ꞌda a.

Mbe ombulanɔ kɔ le ꞌd́a lɛ:

Ma  wia na obe Zaire vɛ̃, wanɔ wa wía sabɛlɛ
tako nɨ, wa futa takoe ɔ nɛ kɛ mbula ꞌd̃a lɛ
be ma ni.

Ma  wia  na be Zaire vƐ̈ ɔ ɑ̈o kartɛ citoyen.
Nɛ ma wè na a ele ma  kɔ yali  ꞌda̍̈a  gɔ.
Ɨala  kɛ wa ba mɔ, nɛ nd᷆e mɔ d̍o kartɛ bina,
wa dɛ sɛ fala !̃ na, mɔ a be Zaíre ní  nɛ

2. Ma wia na be Zaire d̍ɛ mɔ ɑ̍o wala olo nɛ

Ngangga  gɛ,  lɛ̄ zɔ̍a  na wɔ mbḭIıi ma gʉ᷄ kɔ
sila wɨnɔ wena.  T̃akɔ na wa dɛ tonɔ̍̈ꞌda  wa
d̃o  wala  olo  nɛ ̣ní, nɛ wa kŝ̃a na wele hʉ
nmbili hʉ lo, sɛ nɛ ̣lo̍ d̍ɛ to hâ a de.- Na mɛ
ní,  wɨ  sʉnɔ  wâ kp̄a  zɛlɛ tɛ wena, nē wɨ
mbili̍nɔ wa wia we d̍ɛ mɛnɔ vɛ̃ zâ wa  kɔ᷄a  ni
do  mbɨlḭ ̍ꞌd̄a  ẅa. Ki ̍nií  ma  ꞌɑanga le
ꞌd̄anga᷆. ɨ̃

Ma wia na, lɛ kunu sila lɛ datḭ mbili,  lɛ
tɛ d̄è to tabi gbɛ d̄ia gulu we hʉ wele we kɛ
lɛ kpa mbili kɔ̃ a, tabi we kɛ lɛ ı̃ ti a  ź
nɨ  gə- Lɛ  d̄ɛ nı́ nd̄e lɛ ꞌdanga le ꞌda lɛ
Ꞌdanga.

3. Ma wia na be Zaire dɛ to d̍o nga ẅe ꞌ gala
le ꞌd᷆a a, takɔ̍ ma nɛ d̃ɛ  dati.-A̍wɨ̂a we dɛ
tɔa̰, wa f̃ɔ̍, fa mbı̍lı .....

4. K᷄pamɛnɔ d̄o mɛnɔ ngɔ nı᷆ḭ ꞌd̍a lɛ gɛ,  wele
nɛ a  mɔ l̍ɛ.  We duzu ma, ma wia na, lɛ vɛ
lɛ zɔ mɔ tɛ mɛnɔ nḭ̍, ɑ̍ɔ ma ꞌd̄᷄á᷇ngá yala  iko
gə.
