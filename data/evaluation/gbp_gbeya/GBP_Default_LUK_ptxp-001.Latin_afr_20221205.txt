Dedhe Nuûwen nɛ

LUÚŘEË
ɔrQ̂ nƐ mbɛEti

Gɛk wen zu mbɛti kɔ̂ Luke

Wire nɛ vɔra mbɛti nƏ̂ɔƆ ɔ nɛ Luke. À ɔƆ nɛ ga̧sa wizam zɛ̂rƐ̂, nɛ
ganaa ɔ nɛ Zuwif nâ. A̰ vɔra mbɛsti nɔƏ̂ɔ wen usi Dedhe Nuûwen nɛ
ha̧ kpasi kɔ̂ nmang-nmang ha owire nê̂ dhongwaa kɔ̂ Zezi-Kristi,
gan sSɔƆn, owire nɛ gan ɔ nɛ oZuwif nâi. Luke ha ɔra mbɛti  kɔ̂
«DɛƐ̂âmɔ̂dɛv kɔ̂ oBetom» bhɔ. Nɛ tƐ̂ kɔ̧ ombɛti riito nɛ Luke vɔrâi, a̧
tɔ̧wâ wen ha nma wire nɛ gan ɔ nɛ Zuwif nâ go vinaa ha Tevofili.
Luke gan zɔk Zezi nɛ rip ɛ̧ wantɛ nâ, gʼa̧ kidho wen zu dɛƐ̂vâzan kɔ̂
Zezi nɛʼ̂ dhongwaa âkâ owire nɛ zɔkâ Zezi nɛ ripʼrôi sokʼa̧ vɔra nɛ
mbɛtii.

Luke uso Zezi tɛ zan ôrô Bem kɔ̂ wire nɛ tɛvâ wen kid̍hi owire
nɛ vɔwâ dhongwaa kʼôroô sidhai ga wen zam kpasi kƏ̂ wa (Ɔ:lO).
Nʼa̧ usô Zezi tɛ zan ôrô wire nɛ zɔk ala kɔƏ̂ owire nɛ owire zɔk wa
ôr gbɔlɔ wire gai: Owikɔlɛ, okoôo in obem. Luke usô tom nɛ FɛƐ̂ngâ-
Sɔʼ̰̂dan dɛâ zang kƏ̂ omɔ nɛ Zezi dɛvâi bhɔ. Dedhe Nuûwen nɛ Luke
ɔrâ nɛ mbɛtii duro gan ombɛti sɔn nɛ wa ɔra kɔ̧ Mbe-Ndɔkɛi.

Luke ku̧wɔ nângâ vɔɔ mbɛti kʼɛ̧i nɛ dhongwaa bûrô dhong owen
ngɛ̂lĉ̂ng-ngɛ̂lĉ̂ng zu kov kɔ̂ Zezi, kɔƆɔraa nʼa̧ tɔ̧wâ wen zu onma mɔ
nɛ dɛâ mɔ tƐ̂ wese nɛ Zezi ku̧wɔ nângâ tom kʼɛ̧ zû nu nɔ̂ɔ̂i. À̰ tɔ̧wâ
wen dɔ̂ka zu omɔ nɛ Zezi dɛva zû nu nɔ̂ɔ̂i, nʼa̧ tɔ̧wâ wen bhɔ zu fev
kʼa̧ zu sangkarâng te in tûnɔƏ̂wa kʼa̧ kɛngbarâ tɛ ofe.

Tɛ sɔnko mbɛtii, Luke tɔ̧wâ wen zu dhongwaa nɛ Zezi dungɔ
kpa nɛ̂ nmaa in owiwemɔ kʼɛ̧̧ wâ̂â̂ dɔ̂ka-dƏ̂ka kɔraa nʼa̧ tunɔ ha tɛ̂
fei. Luke tɔ̧wâ wen bhɔ zu dhongwaa nɛ Zezi dhafa owiwemɔ kʼʼɛ̧
wen usi Dedhe Nuûwen ha owirei. Kɔraa nʼa̧ tɔ̧wâ wen zu dhongwaa
nɛ Nzapa baa nƐ̂ Zezi tɛ ripzan mɛi.
