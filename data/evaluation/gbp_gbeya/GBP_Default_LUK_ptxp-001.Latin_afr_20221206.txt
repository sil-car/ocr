LU᷇ŘË
ƆrɑQ̂ nƐ mb̍Ɛti

Gɛk wen zu mbɛti̍ kɔ̂ Luke

Wire nɛ vɔra mbɛti nƏ̂Ɔ ɔ nɛ Luke. ̰ Ɔ nɛ ga̧sa wizam zɛ̂rɛƐ̂, nɛ
ganaa Ɔ nɛ Zuwif nâ. A̧ vɔra mbɛti nɔ̂ɔ wen usi Dedh̍e Nuûwen nɛ
ha̧ kpasi kɔ̂ nmang-nmang ha owire nɛ̂ dhongwaa kɔ̂ Zezi-Kristi,
gan  sƆn, Owire ns gan ɔ nƐ OZuwif nâi. Luke ha vƆra mbɛti kɔ̂
«DɛƐ̂vâ̂mɔ̂d̍ɛv kƏ̂ oBetom» bhƆ. Nɛc tƐ̂ kɔ̧ ombɛti riito nɛ Luke vƆɔrâi, a̧
tɔ̧wâ̂ wen ha nma wire nɛ gan Ɔ nɛ Zuwif nâ go vinaa ha Tevofili̍.
Luke gan zƆɔk Zezi nɛ rip ɛ̧ wantɛ nâ, gʼa̧ kidho wen zu dɛƐ̂vâzan kɔ̂
Zezi nɛ̂ dhongwaa âkâ owire nɛ zɔkâ Zezi nɛ ripʼr᷆i sokʼa̧ vɔra nɛ
mbɛtii.

Luke uso Zezi tɛ zan ᷆rô BBem kɔ̂ wire nɛ tcvâ wen kid̍hi owire
nɛ vɔwâ̂ dhongwaa kʼôr sidhavi ga wen zam kpasi kƏ̂ wa (ƏƆ:lO).
Nʼa̧ usô Zezi tc zan ôr᷆ wire nɛ zɔk ala kɔ̂ oOowire nɛ owire zɔk wa
r̀ gbƆl̍Ɔɔ wire gai: Owikɔlɛ, ok᷆o in obem. Luke usoô tom nɛ FɛƐʼ̂ngâ-
Sɔ̣̂dan d̍cvâ zang kƏ̂ omƆ nɛ Zezi dcvâi bh̀Ɔ. Ded̍he Nûwen ns Luke
vƆrâ̂ nɛ mbɛtii̍ d̍uro gan ombɛti sɔn nɛ wa vɔra kɔ̧ Mbe-Nd̍ɔkɛi.

Luke kuụ̧wƆ nângâ vɔɔ mbɛti kʼɛ̧i̍ nc dhongwaa bûrô dhOng oOowen
ngɛ̂lƐĉ̂ng-ngɛ̂lɛĉ̂ng zu kov kɔ̂ Zezi, kƆraa nʼa̧ tɔ̣wâ wen zu onma mɔƆ
nɛ dɛvâ mɔƆ tɛ̂ wese nc Zezi ku̧wɔ nâ̂ngâ tom kʼɛ̧ zû nu nɔ̂ɔ̂i. ̰ tɔ̣wâ
wen d̍ɔƏ̂ka zu omƆ nɛ Zezi d̍ɛva zû nu nɔ̂ɔ̂i, nʼa̧ tɔ̣wâ wen bhƆ zu fev
kʼa̧ zu sangkarâng te in tûnɔ̂wa kʼa̧ kcngbarâ tc ofe.

Tɛ sɔnkoOv mbɛtii, Luke tɔ̣wâ wen zu d́h̍Ongwaa nɛ Zezi d̍ungƆ
kpa nɛ̂ nmaa in owiwemɔƆ kʼɛ̧ wââ dɔ̂ka-d̍ɔƏ̂ka kƆɔraa nʼa̧ tunƆ ha tƐ̂
fei. Luke tɔ̧̣wâ wen bhƆ zu d̍h̍ongwaa nɛ Zezi dhafa owiwemɔ kʼɛ̧
wen usi Ded̍he Nûwen ha owirei. Kɔraa nʼa̧ tɔ̣wâ wen zu dh̍Ongwaa
nɛƐ Nzapa baa nƐ̂ Zezi tɛ ripzan mɛci.
