Kidhe na ngono manda ge ha vaka ma, yoge emvi
nɔn niye desmalɛ, de zaa tarə manɛ. Niye desmalɛ
ma bhoro yoge tɛ: “ Dhi usi gidi ma tu”, na yoge egi
zagu kovwu nzili tɛ: “ Kori kal rhubha, de zaa, zi ze
gara zɛrə, ze zʼas kusu gidi de zaa gunu”. Yoge uni
dhovo deri nɛ. Kazə nɔn, Kokudho wo vɔyɔ ngono
